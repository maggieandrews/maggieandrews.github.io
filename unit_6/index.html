About Us

Sticks+Stones is a small woodshop, based out of Oakland, California that makes custom furniture and home goods products that mix both industrial and rustic styles. All of our products are hand made with love and are built to last a lifetime.

Hand Made in Oakland, CA

All of our products are hand crafted in our Oakland woodshop. If you feel like checking out how we make our products, stop by and say hi!

Made from Reclaimed Wood

We love the idea that something new can be made from something old. The wood that we use for our furniture is sourced from environmentally recycling mills.

Custom Furniture Design

Do you need a custom piece of furniture made specifically to meet certain demensions. We offer custom designs where we will work with you to design a fully custom peice of furniture that you will love!
